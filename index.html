Daan Forest Park

🚇 MRT: Daan Park Station
From Hotel: Take Blue Line from Nanjing Fuxing → Zhongxiao Fuxing (1 stop) → Transfer to Brown Line → Daan Park (2 stops)
📍 Learn More: Click here for details about Daan Forest Park
🌳 Morning Walk: Start at the main entrance, follow the pond circuit for bird watching
📸 Photo Spots: Traditional pavilion by the pond, bamboo grove area
🦆 Nature: Look for egrets, ducks, and squirrels - very active in morning

Huashan 1914 Creative Park

🚇 MRT: Zhongxiao Xinsheng Station
From Yongkang: Walk to Dongmen Station → Blue/Orange Line → Zhongxiao Xinsheng (3 stops)
📍 Learn More: Click here for details about Huashan 1914 Creative Park
🎨 Art Galleries: Multiple exhibition spaces with rotating contemporary art shows (some free, some ticketed)
🏭 Historic Architecture: Japanese-era sake brewery buildings from 1914, now restored
🌳 Green Spaces: Tree-lined paths and outdoor installations, perfect for strolling
🛍️ Design Shops: Unique Taiwanese design products, crafts, and creative souvenirs
📸 Photo Ops: Industrial-chic backdrop with ivy-covered walls and vintage machinery

Return to Hotel

📚 Optional: Browse Eslite Underground Bookstore (if time permits)

Essential Tips

  • Buy EasyCard at airport (NT$100 card + add NT$400 value)
  • Download "Taipei Metro" app for offline MRT navigation
  • Carry small cash - many snack vendors don't take cards
  • Bring reusable water bottle - free refill stations in MRT
  • October weather: 24-28°C, humid - dress light and carry umbrella
  • Most signs have English, but screenshot addresses in Chinese
